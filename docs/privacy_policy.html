Privacy Policy for LearnFlutter

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Introduction

UK Solutions ("us", "we", or "our") operates the LearnFlutter mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information.

LearnFlutter is a Flutter learning application that operates entirely locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Store your learning progress on our servers
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require account registration or login
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Request unnecessary permissions

Data Storage

All learning progress, favorites, bookmarks, and other app data are stored locally on your device only. This means:

  • Your learning progress remains private on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • Your data is not accessible to us or anyone else
  • You have complete control over your information
  • Data is not backed up to cloud services by the app

Permissions

LearnFlutter may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To store your learning progress and favorites locally on your device
  • Internet: To load learning content, documentation links, and external resources

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's functionality.

Third-Party Content

LearnFlutter may contain links to external websites and resources:

  • Official Flutter Documentation: Links to flutter.dev and pub.dev
  • External Learning Resources: Links to tutorials, videos, and articles
  • Code Examples: References to GitHub repositories and code samples

These external sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

LearnFlutter does not use any third-party analytics or advertising networks that may collect information used to identify you.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security
  • We recommend using device lock features (PIN, password, fingerprint, face ID)
  • If you uninstall the app, all data will be permanently deleted
  • We do not have access to recover your data if it's lost

Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you maintain complete control:

  • All data belongs to you
  • You can delete all data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • No data is retained by us after app deletion
  • No data is shared with any third parties

Content Attribution

LearnFlutter provides educational content about Flutter development:

  • All Flutter-related content is referenced from official sources
  • Code examples are for educational purposes only
  • We respect intellectual property rights and provide proper attribution
  • Users should verify information with official Flutter documentation
