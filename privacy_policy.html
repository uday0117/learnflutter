Information Collection and Use

We do not directly collect any personal information.

LearnFlutter is a Flutter learning application that operates primarily locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information directly

  • Store your learning progress on our servers
  • Require account registration or login
  • Request unnecessary permissions

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: This app displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Ad Interaction: Information about how you interact with ads
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (if enabled)
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to serve relevant ads

AdMob Privacy Policy: For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

We use AdMob to support free access to educational content. Ad revenue helps maintain and improve the app.

Data Storage

All learning progress, favorites, bookmarks, and other app data are stored locally on your device only. This means:

  • Your learning progress remains private on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • Your data is not accessible to us or anyone else
  • You have complete control over your information
  • Data is not backed up to cloud services by the app

LearnFlutter may request the following permissions:

We only request permissions that are essential for the app's functionality.
